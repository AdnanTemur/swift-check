# SwiftCheck Privacy Policy

SwiftCheck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All speed test traffic goes directly between your browser and
speed.cloudflare.com. No results, IP addresses, or usage data
are sent to any server operated by the developer. Test results
are stored locally on your device in chrome.storage.local and
never leave your machine.
